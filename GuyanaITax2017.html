Some Notes

  • NIS Deduction Explanation
  • If you earn 60,000 less/month OR 720,00O or less/year, no tax is deducted
  • Non-taxable income is set at 60,000/month if you earn =<180,000/month
  • If you earn >60,000/month and =< 180,000/month, 28% tax is deducted
  • If you earn >720,000/year and =< 2,160,000/year, 28% tax is deducted
  • Non-taxable income is set at 1/3 of gross if you earn more than 180,000/month or 2,160,000/year
  • If you earn more than 180,000/month or 2,160,000/year your tax is calculated in 2 parts:
    • 28% of 120,000 + 40% of the rest, of taxable income OR
    • 28% of (12*120,000 or 1,440,000) + 40% of the rest
  • NIS is NON-Taxable and is deducted before final tax is calculated
